Raid AlWabil, SOCPA

Owner

raid.alwabil@reyada.sa

Raid has over 10 years of experience in the Assurance (Audit) Service of EY, PWC, and KPMG Riyadh Offices. He has audit experience in the manufacturing, retail, real estate, hospitality, government, and transportation industries, in private and listed, high-profile companies.

After leaving EY in 2017, Raid joined the Arabian Agricultural Services Company (ARASCO) as deputy Finance manager. From January 2021 onwards, Raid was appointed as the General Manager of Finance for Saudi Customs and subsequently for the General Authority of Civil Aviation.

For the above entities, Raid has developed and led the finance departments, established KPIs to evaluate the performance of the entities operations, provided detailed feasibility studies for expansion of operations, including production lines, both in Saudi Arabia and abroad. He also supervised the complex accounting transition from cash basis to accrual basis for the two Government Authorities mentioned above.

Raid is a licensed member of the Saudi Organization for Chartered and Professional Accountants (license #548) and holds a Bachelor of Science in Accounting from King Saud University (KSA).

Navigate the complex terrain of regulations and financial intricacies with a team of experienced professionals.
