Assurance Services

In audit and assurance, the numbers tell a story. They recognize achievements and address challenges, while helping show you how to create a solid base to achieve future goals.

With Reyada, you will gain clarity on what, how, and why of change, helping you to proactively take action.

Our leading Assurance Services comply with the
International Standards on Auditing endorsed in KSA.
We also adhere to the International Financial Reporting Standards, including the ones for small and medium-
sized entities that are endorsed in KSA. In addition, we follow other standards and pronouncements endorsed
by the Saudi Organization for Chartered and Professional Accountants (SOCPA).

Our assurance services include:

  • Audit of annual and special purpose financial statements
  • Review of interim financial statements
  • Agreed-upon procedures for financial and non-financial information
  • Limited review report

Performance of cash-basis audits in accordance with Ministry of Finance regulations and other regulatory endorsements in KSA

Advisory Services

At Reyada, we understand the challenges of progress in today's ever-changing business world. That's why our advisors, with experience from the Big Four Accounting firms, are here to provide you with tailored solutions and expert guidance.

With Reyada, you will work smarter and make better decisions.

Whether you are looking to exceed customer expectations, navigate regulations, increase efficiency, or achieve profitable growth, we have the strategies and recommendations to help you succeed with our financial, governance and operational consulting alike.

Our financial advisory services include:

  • Internal Audit (IA) services
  • Accounting records and books
  • Policies and procedure manuals (PPM)
  • Transition of International Financial Reporting Standard for SMEs to International Financial Reporting standards (IFRS), as endorsed in KSA
  • Reviewing and implementing IFRS standards, including IFRS 9 (Expected Credit Loss Module), IFRS 15 (Revenue from Contracts and Customers), IFRS 16 (Leases), and IAS 19 (Employee Benefits)

Our operational services include:

  • Corporate Finance Advisory Services which includes:
    • Financial and tax due diligence (buy side, sell side)
    • Business valuation
    • Purchase price allocation (PPA)
    • Goodwill impairment testing
  • Development and design of a comprehensive Corporate Governance system of rules, practices, and processes to assist the board of directors in leading and managing a business

Zakat and Tax Services

We provide a range of services tailored to both local and global tax regulations, including zakat-related assistance.

Put your trust in us for all your tax needs.

Our comprehensive tax offerings include:

  • Zakat and tax compliance services
  • Preparation and filing of:
    • Annual zakat and income tax returns
    • Monthly and annual withholding tax returns
    • Monthly and quarterly value-added tax (VAT) returns
  • Compliance with transfer pricing requirements
  • Replying to zakat and tax queries and attending meetings with authorities
  • Zakat and tax dispute resolution services
  • Drafting and submitting appeal memos
  • Representing the companies before committees
  • Submitting appeal to tax committees including all dispute committees
  • Attending hearings alongside your organization

Navigate the complex terrain of regulations and financial intricacies with a team of experienced professionals.
